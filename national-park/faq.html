Hours

  • * Interpretive programs available year-round.
  • Visitor Center
    • Open daily 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
    • Closed New Year's Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day.
  • NOTE: Meeting room in Administration Office available all year by reservations.
  • Campground/Picnic Area:
    • Open all year
    • Note: Some restrooms and bathhouses are closed on Dec. 1 - March 1
  • Cabins: Available year-round
  • Cafe:
    • Peak Season
      • Friday before Memorial Day Weekend-Labor Day
      • Thursday-Monday 10 a.m.-6 p.m.
      • Closed Tuesdays and Wednesdays
    • Off-Peak Season
      • Tuesday after Labor Day to Thursday before Memorial Day Weekend
      • Closed
  • Park Store / Boat Rental:
    • March 1 - Memorial Day Weekend 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
    • Memorial Day Weekend - Labor Day 9 a.m. - 6 p.m.
    • Labor Day - Nov. 15, 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
    • Closed Mondays & Tuesdays
  • Pool:
    • Memorial Day Weekend-Labor Day
    • Wednesday-Sunday 12 p.m.-6 p.m.
    • Closed Mondays and Tuesdays (except for Memorial Day, Labor Day, and Fourth of July when required)
    • Pool parties may be booked outside of normal business hours if staffing allows. Please call to inquire.
